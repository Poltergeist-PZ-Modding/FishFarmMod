description=[list]
description=[*] Build a farm: This requires to read the magazine (can be disabled), a hammer and building materials. Players can build them on empty water squares near the shore.
description=[*] Fish Food (compost): You add food to the farms for the farm to produce items, fish eat it (based on the compost speed) and the farm produces fish.
description=[*] There are 3 different farm types that produce different items. The bait farm to get bait fish, the crab farm to get crabs / lobsters, the shrimp farm that produces shrimps / shells.
description=[*] New tasty recipes to cook the farm items.
